SnapLink © - About Us

Welcome to SnapLink - Your Free and Simple Image Hosting Solution!



SnapLink was created by a team of four backend developers: YOUNESSE25, MASTAX, MMMBENCHAHYD and ZAKARIIIA as part of our portfolio project in the ALX Africa Software Engineering Program.


Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use platform where users can upload and share images effortlessly, with support for direct links, GitHub, and HTML markdown formats.


Why Choose SnapLink?


  • - Free & Unlimited Uploads: No limits on the number of images (max size: 10MB per image).
  • - Direct Links for Developers: Get direct URLs, markdown links, and GitHub-compatible links instantly.
  • - Secure & Reliable Hosting: Your images are stored on Amazon Cloud with top-tier security measures.
  • - Permanent Image Hosting: Your images will remain online as long as SnapLink is operational.
  • - User Privacy First: We never share your images or your informations with third parties and enforce strict content policies.

At SnapLink, we believe in open, accessible, and secure image hosting for everyone. Thank you for using SnapLink!
